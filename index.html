About Elevate

Elevate Trailer House is founded by award-winning sound designer and re-recording mixer Teddy Teske. With a career spanning over a decade, Teddy began in Los Angeles working on a small independent films, which led to credits on major studio features and large-scale theatrical campaigns. We specialize in custom music, cinematic sound design, SFX libraries, and music library micro-licensing, while also offering full AV marketing and trailer campaign services. The sounds found in our libraries are derived from original material created for feature films and campaigns, including unused and unreleased sound design from projects we’ve worked on. We collaborate 1-on-1 with directors and producers through dedicated Zoom sessions to understand the story, tone, and emotional intent, ensuring every project serves the narrative first. Whether the project calls for custom music and sound design or a fully realized trailer campaign, we collaborate closely with filmmakers and producers to translate creative intent into powerful, story-driven sound.
